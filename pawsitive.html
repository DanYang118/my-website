PAWsitive

Background

PAWsitive’s orthopaedic dog bed is a premium product with strong functional benefits, yet its value proposition is not effectively communicated to the target demographic. As a result, potential customers admire the product’s aesthetics but hesitate to purchase.

The Goal

To increase customer confi dence and engagement by clearly articulating the product’s quality, emotional signifi cance, and practical value, ultimately driving conversions and brand loyalty.

Reflection

This project highlighted the importance of aligning UX design with marketing communication. Clear storytelling and emotional design help users understand the value of premium products, building trust and strengthening engagement with the brand.

Core Competencies Applied

Strategic Planning
Market analysis, value proposition, and roadmap definition.
Brand Identity
Tone of voice, visual storytelling, and emotional design.
Product Thinking
Feature prioritization based on user impact vs. effort.
Emotional Storytelling
Show how products improve pets’ everyday lives.Shift messaging from price to care.
Visual Design
The visual system was designed to communicate warmth and premium quality.
