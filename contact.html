Hi, I'm Dan

I’m a UX Designer based in Calgary, passionate about creating digital products that are intuitive, inclusive, and meaningful. I believe good design starts with people. By understanding user needs, behaviors, and pain points—and combining this with market insights—I aim to design experiences that are clear, accessible, and enjoyable to use. My learning journey at SAIT has strengthened my design thinking, research, and collaboration skills, while also shaping me into a thoughtful problem solver. Beyond the classroom, I bring empathy, patience, and creativity from my life experiences into every project I take on.

Feel free to reach out for opportunities, collaborations, or just a chat.
