Visual design

Visual design is about creating clarity, emotion, and identity through colors, typography, and composition. In these projects, I explored branding, packaging, illustration, and advertising design. I learned how to translate abstract brand values into visual systems, design for both digital and print contexts, and craft visuals that are consistent, appealing, and memorable.

Visual design also directly supports my UX work. By sharpening my eye for detail, improving my sense of hierarchy and readability, and building consistent design systems, I am able to create interfaces that are not only functional, but also intuitive and visually engaging for users.
