YASAI Ramen

Background

Green Designs developed a business proposal for a restaurant called Yasai Ramen, which specializes in plant-based cuisine. To help the brand stand out in the increasingly competitive plant-based food market, this project proposed a marketing campaign centered on transit advertising.

The Goal

Through innovative graphic design and messaging that promotes a green lifestyle, the campaign aims to position Yasai Ramen as a leading brand in the growing plant-based food industry.

Core Competencies Applied

Marketing Strategy
Developing a "Green Lifestyle" transit campaign, focusing on the 3-second rule for high-speed visual impact and brand positioning.
Brand Identity
Aligning "Green Lifestyle" values with a premium, plant-based dining experience.
Transit Poster Design
Concept development for high-traffic public spaces
Visual Communication
High-contrast layouts designed for quick readability from a distance.
