Suit Store Checkout Flow Redesign

Background

Many e-commerce sites have long, unclear checkout flows that frustrate users and lower conversion rates.

Goal

Streamline the checkout process to make it faster, simpler, and more transparent.

Solution

Researched existing checkout patterns, identified best practices, and designed a clean step-by-step flow with progress indicators and clear order review.

Design Process

  • Competitive Analysis: Researched competitor apps (MTailor, Tom Tailor, Sumissura, Amazon) to identify best practices in checkout and customization flows.
  • Problem Investigation: Highlighted issues such as unclear progress indicators, lack of trust signals, and overly complex review steps.
  • Wireframes: Sketched low-fidelity screens to explore layout options and step-by-step flow.
