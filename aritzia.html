Aritzia App Research

Background

Research on how Aritzia shoppers use digital channels and what they expect from a branded app.

Goal

Understand how Aritzia customers shop digitally, identify unmet needs, and propose mobile app features that enhance engagement, loyalty, and brand growth.

User Research Methods

  • Survey: Collected responses from Aritzia shoppers about shopping habits, preferences, and frustrations.
  • User Interviews: Conducted 1:1 interviews to gather deeper insights on pain points and expectations.
  • Empathy Map: Visualized what users say, think, feel, and do during the shopping journey to uncover motivations and frustrations.
  • Project Process Map: Outlined key stages from survey design → data analysis → insights → ideation → concept proposal.
