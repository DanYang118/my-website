BB4CK

Background

BB4CK is committed to ensuring every child is free from hunger. However, the organization faces bottlenecks in long-term growth potential and digital reach. To ensure the sustainability of this vital service, the brand urgently needs to engage more young audiences.

The Goal

This project aims to modernize BB4CK’s recruitment through a digital-first strategy, using shareable TikTok content and targeted campus advertising to engage Calgary’s next generation.

Core Competencies Applied

Visual Storytelling
Using empathetic imagery and clean typography to communicate urgent social needs without compromising dignity.
Marketing Strategy
Developing local awareness campaigns centered on "Local Impact," encouraging Calgarians to support their neighbors.
Market Research
Conducting competitive auditing and situational analysis to identify gaps in the plant-based food market and non-profit volunteer engagement.
Trend Analysis
Monitoring digital media shifts and consumption habits to adapt traditional non-profit models for TikTok and modern mobile platforms.
Audience Analysis
Defining user personas and behavioral patterns to pivot marketing efforts from senior-led demographics to Gen Z and university students.
